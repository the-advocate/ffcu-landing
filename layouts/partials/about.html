Membership Eligibility

If you live, work, worship, or attend school in West Feliciana, East Feliciana, Pointe Coupee, or the northern portion of East Baton Rouge (Zachary & Baker) parishes, you are eligible and welcome to join Feliciana Federal Credit Union. We are a “Community Credit Union” with two convenient locations to serve you. Tell all your friends and neighbors to stop by and become part of our credit union family.
